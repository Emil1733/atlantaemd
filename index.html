Pool Abandonment vs. Full Removal in Atlanta

Understand your options for Atlanta pool removal in Georgia's unique clay environment.

Partial Removal (Abandonment)

The pool shell remains in the ground. The top 2-3 feet are removed, drainage holes are punched, and the area is backfilled with dirt.

  • ✓ Lower immediate Atlanta pool removal cost
  • ⚠ Backfill is not "buildable" grade
  • ⚠ Must be disclosed during property resale
  • ⚠ Higher risk of sinkholes in Georgia clay

Full Engineered Removal

The entire pool structure is removed. This is the gold standard for Atlanta pool removal projects.

  • ✓ Property is declared 100% buildable
  • ✓ Zero long-term settlement or sinkhole risk
  • ✓ No disclosure required for future home buyers
  • ✓ Guaranteed stable in Metro Atlanta's red clay
