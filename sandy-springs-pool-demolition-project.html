Solving Sandy Springs Specific Hurdle

How we protected a $40,000 stamped concrete driveway during heavy hauling.

The Problem: Driveway Integrity

The only machine access was over a masterfully stamped concrete driveway. A standard dump truck could have easily cracked the structural slab, costing the homeowner tens of thousands in damage.

The Solution: Rubber-Track Strategy

We utilized specialized rubber-tracked loaders and laid down double-layered plywood "roads" across the driveway. We also used smaller 10-yard dump trucks to distribute the weight safely.
