Overcoming Marietta-Specific Challenges

Real projects never go perfectly. Here’s how we solved them.

Challenge: Limited Side-Yard Access

The homeowner's property had only a 48-inch clearance between the garage and the fence. Standard excavators couldn't reach the pool area without removing the neighbor's mature trees.

Solution: We utilized a specialized 36" mini-excavator and power buggies to ferry material without disturbing existing landscaping.

Challenge: Heavy Georgia Red Clay

West Marietta is notorious for high-plasticity clay that retains water. Filling a pool with standard topsoil would lead to a "spongy" yard and eventual sinkholes.

Solution: We implemented a layered drainage base with vibration-compacted structural fill, verified to 95% Proctor density.
