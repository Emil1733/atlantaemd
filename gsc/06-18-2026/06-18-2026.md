## 🚦 2. The Indexation Bottleneck (The Root Cause)

Despite our comprehensive structural overhaul on May 31 (rebuilding the sitemap with all 32 pages, fixing internal links, and pinging the Indexing API), **Google is still refusing to crawl and index 30 out of 32 pages.**

Only the **Homepage (`/`)** and **Buford (`/buford-pool-removal.html`)** are indexed and generating impressions. The other 30 pages remain in a `[NEUTRAL] - Discovered, currently not indexed` state. 

### Why is this happening?
This is a textbook case of **Crawl Budget Starvation** due to a lack of **Domain Authority**. Google has read the sitemap and knows the pages exist, but because the domain `atlantapoolremoval.com` is brand new and has **zero external backlinks**, Google's algorithm does not prioritize spending crawl budget to render and index the deep pages. 

The on-page SEO is flawless (1,800+ words per page, WebP images, strong internal siloing), but Google needs an external "trust signal" to validate the site's existence before it will unleash its crawler.
