About Atlanta Pool Removal Pros

Our team is dedicated to providing high-trust Atlanta pool removal services across Northern Georgia.

Dedicated to Real Results for Atlanta Homeowners

We started Atlanta Pool Removal Pros with a single goal: help families in Marietta, Sandy Springs, and throughout the metro area reclaim space that was once tied up in expensive, unused swimming pools.

Our Team and Experience: With decade-plus experience in residential excavation, our team understands the geological challenges of Georgia—specifically the ways our clay soil needs to be managed for perfect compaction. We aren't just here to demo; we're here to restore the property's potential.

We have handled local projects that range from small suburban lots to larger residential estates in Alpharetta. No job is too small or too technically challenging for our core group of specialists.
