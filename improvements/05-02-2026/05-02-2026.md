Implemented a multi-layer security protocol to protect the platform and its lead-capture integrity.
*   **Secrets Migration**: Created a secure `_secrets/` vault and migrated `.env.local` and `gsc_credentials.json` out of the web-root to prevent public exposure.
*   **HoneyPot Spam Defense**: Injected forensic HoneyPot traps into the **Contact Form** and **Diagnostic Calculator** across the entire network to eliminate bot-driven lead pollution.
*   **Content Security Policy (CSP)**: Hardened the main portal with a CSP meta-header to block unauthorized scripts and prevent XSS attacks.
*   **Script Re-Mapping**: Updated `gsc_manager.js` and `gsc_audit.js` to point to the new secure vault, ensuring 100% technical continuity.
